## The Big Shift

Most early product work still begins in a messy way:

- Ideas live in scattered notes,
- Requirements stay vague too long,
- PM artifacts get written late,
- Teams debate structure instead of moving,
- Architecture and stories become manual cleanup work.

PM Architect AI changes that loop.

It gives users an AI-guided discovery flow, then converts the clarified product idea into a structured workspace with PRDs, personas, user stories, technical architecture, diagrams, and project persistence.

The "wow" is not just that AI writes a document.
The "wow" is that product thinking becomes a reusable workspace instead of a one-off brainstorming thread.

> From rough product instinct to a structured delivery-ready workspace.

## Why It Feels Different

1. **Discover** the idea through guided AI conversation.
2. **Generate** the core PM artifacts in one coherent flow.
3. **Continue** refining and managing those artifacts inside a persistent workspace.

This repository is a public product-information and support hub for the hosted PM Architect AI product. It does not contain the application source code.

## What You Get

- `AI Discovery Flow` for clarifying goals, users, and scope before generation
- `PM Artifact Suite` for PRDs, personas, stories, technical architecture, and diagrams
- `Persistent Workspace` with dashboard, projects, and settings
- `Delivery Shape` that helps product thinking move closer to execution
- `Knowledge Sync` through Synapse integration for pushing generated artifacts outward

## Who It Is For

- Founders turning rough ideas into product direction
- Product managers who need a faster first-draft system
- Builders who want clearer specs before development
- Teams that want to push product artifacts into a broader knowledge workflow

## What PM Architect AI Does

- Publishes a public landing page for the product
- Supports login and authenticated workspace access
- Stores and displays projects in a dashboard
- Generates PM artifacts such as PRDs, personas, stories, architecture, and diagrams
- Provides settings for account and Synapse integration

## Why It Exists

PM Architect AI is not trying to replace product judgment.

It exists for a narrower and more practical problem:

> "A team has an idea, but needs a much faster way to structure it into useful product artifacts before development momentum is lost."

That is why the product feels more like an AI PM workspace than a general writing tool.

## Why People Remember It

- It makes PM output feel immediate instead of delayed
- It compresses discovery and documentation into one flow
- It bridges ideation with structured execution artifacts
- It gives teams something they can refine, not just admire once

## Privacy Model

PM Architect AI is a hosted web product.

High-level data behavior:

- visitors can browse the landing page publicly,
- authenticated users can access their workspace and saved projects,
- project content and generated artifacts may be stored server-side to support the experience,
- external AI and integration providers may process content depending on active configuration.

See [docs/PRIVACY.md](docs/PRIVACY.md) for the short public privacy summary.
